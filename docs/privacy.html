PaddleRats Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

What We Collect

PaddleRats collects the following information:

  • Name — to identify players in session invitations and confirmations.
  • Phone number — to send and receive SMS messages related to session coordination.
  • Email address — for account login (session organizers only).
  • PTI rating — sourced from publicly available APTA league data to help organizers find compatible players.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Send SMS invitations to platform tennis sessions.
  • Send booking confirmations when a session is filled.
  • Send expiration notices when an invitation times out.
  • Allow session organizers to manage their contacts and groups.

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose unrelated to coordinating platform tennis sessions.

SMS Messaging

By participating in PaddleRats, you may receive SMS messages including session invitations, booking confirmations, and reminders. Message frequency varies based on session activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out of receiving messages.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is shared only with:

  • Twilio — our SMS delivery provider, which processes phone numbers and message content to deliver texts.
  • Other players — your name and PTI rating are visible to other players in sessions you join.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secured database. We use industry-standard practices to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon request, we will remove your personal information from our systems.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion, contact us at privacy@paddlerats.com.
